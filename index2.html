what is this website?

pearlocean is my home on the internet, an online journal/writing blog of sorts. here you will find my musings and writing on various subjects, including my hobbies, as well as borderline thoughts. ever since i got diagnosed with bpd, i find that laying down my thoughts helps me understand my brain more. and i also love talking about my various interests in hopes that someone else would pick them up too! hence this website.

introduction

i'm just an eighteen year old girl with a penchant for web design and cutesy things in general. you can refer to me as lain, which is an alias.
